Allah said in the Qur'an:
(أَعُوْذُ بِاللّٰهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطٰنِ الرَّجِيْم)
ٱلَّذِينَ يُنفِقُونَ أَمْوَٰلَهُم بِٱلَّيْلِ وَٱلنَّهَارِ سِرًّا وَعَلَانِيَةً فَلَهُمْ أَجْرُهُمْ عِندَ رَبِّهِمْ وَلَا خَوْفٌ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا هُمْ يَحْزَنُونَ
(البقرة: ٢٧٤)

Those who spend their wealth (in Allah's cause) by night and day, in secret and in public, they shall have their reward with their Lord. On them shall be no fear, nor shall they grieve.
(Al-Baqarah: 274)

Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) said:
The believer's shade on the Day of Resurrection will be his charity.
(AtTirmidhi, 604)

Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) also said:
Every act of goodness is charity.
(Sahih Muslim, 496)

In shaa Allah, your humble donations will be beneficial for the Deen and the Ummah.

About us

Established in the late 70's, our masjid and community has grown tremendously. We have many programs and events to match all generations. Programs are conducted in English & Bengali, to cater the older crowds.

Feel free to visit the Masjid.
